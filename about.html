About us

Entangled Solutions is an innovation agency for education. Our services include strategic consulting, program design, marketing strategy, business model development and implementation planning.

Entangled Solutions was founded in 2015 and is based in San Francisco. Our core team is a group of entrepreneurs with deep experience in product development and organizational change in education. We use our startup connections to bring cutting edge technology to the academic world.
