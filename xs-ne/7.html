Replaced its long annual reports with dynamic infographics

The University of Florida’s annual reports highlight its wide-range of activities each fiscal year. In support of its "Going Green" philosophy, the Career Resource Center has made a concerted effort to reduce its overall carbon footprint and nowadays produces an online version of its annual report. This enables it to make its data more visible for its stakeholders.
