Careers

Higher Education is undergoing the most rapid period of change in its history. To stay relevant, traditional institutions are moving towards new instructional models and launching new types of programs. The best results are often achieved when internal innovation teams are partnering with mission-aligned private companies to catalyze, plan, implement, support, and scale strategic initiatives.

Founded in 2015 and based in San Francisco, Entangled Solutions is a full-service agency that supports education innovators at each stage, from concept development and planning to implementation and scaling. Its services include strategic consulting, initiative design, implementation planning, fundraising support, program development, faculty training, enrollment marketing, and organization creation.

Entangled Solutions is located in downtown San Francisco (at Galvanize SF). Some of our positions are office-based, other are remote.
