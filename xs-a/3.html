Yury Lifshits
Managing Director

Yury Lifshits is the Managing Director at Entangled Solutions. Throughout his career he was an instructor at K-12, undergraduate, graduate, and continuous education programs as well as an education technology developer, entrepreneur, and consultant.

Prior to starting Entangled Solutions, Yury led two education technology startups, a large-scale hybrid bootcamp program in Eastern Europe and one of the first mobile course creation platforms. Earlier, Yury was a data scientist at Caltech and Yahoo! Labs and won two gold medals at the International Math Olympiad.
