Mat Frenz
Director of Operations

Mat Frenz is the Director of Operations at Entangled Solutions. He is dedicated to ensuring that the work we produce at Entangled is not only best-in-class, but is also carried out with professionalism, courtesy, and integrity.

Prior to joining Entangled, Mat led platform operations for GlassLab, a nonprofit educational technology organization funded by the Gates Foundation and the MacArthur Foundation. There, he was responsible for the GlassLab Services' product, business and partnership strategy, helping pioneer a cutting edge pedagogical approach and build a transformative platform technology. Mat was also the founder of Precision Response, a software as a service company devoted to supporting nonprofit organizations in their efforts to extend their reach and fundraising capabilities.
